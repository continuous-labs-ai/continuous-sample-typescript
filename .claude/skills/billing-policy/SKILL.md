# Acme billing policy (authoritative)

Apply these exact terms. Do not substitute general knowledge or assumptions.

## Refunds

- **Monthly plans:** refundable within **14 days** of the charge. After 14 days,
  no refund — the customer can cancel to stop the next renewal.
- **Annual plans:** **prorated refund within 30 days** of purchase. After 30
  days, annual plans are **non-refundable**.
- Cancelling does **not** trigger a refund for unused time on a monthly plan;
  access simply continues until the end of the paid period.

## Plan changes (proration)

- **Upgrades** (e.g. Basic → Pro) take effect **immediately** and are
  **prorated** — the customer is charged the difference for the remainder of the
  current cycle right away.
- **Downgrades** (e.g. Pro → Basic) take effect at the **next billing cycle**.
  They are **not** refunded or prorated mid-cycle; the customer keeps the higher
  tier until the current period ends.

## Seats

- **Adding seats:** prorated and charged immediately.
- **Removing seats:** **credited to the next invoice**, not refunded to the card.

## Free trial

- **14 days**, and a **credit card is required** to start.
- Auto-converts to a paid subscription at the end of the trial unless cancelled
  before it ends.

## Coupons & discounts

- **One discount per subscription.** Coupons **do not stack** with the annual
  discount; the customer keeps whichever discount is larger.

## Pausing

- **Monthly plans** can be paused for **up to 3 months**. Billing is suspended
  while paused and resumes automatically when the pause ends.

## Cancellation

- Access continues until the **end of the current paid period**; cancellation
  only stops future renewals.
- No partial refund for unused time, **except** an annual plan cancelled within
  its 30-day refund window.
